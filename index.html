About Me

I'm an Electronics and Communications Engineering student, with a solid background in the fields of Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Software Engineering, Embedded Systems, Robotics and CS.

Two years experience in building ROVs , resulting in the production of 2 ROVs, winning an honorary prize and being one of the technical support engineering team responsible of building playgrounds and assisting in judging in the Regional ROV Competition in Egypt.

Three years Android Development experience while implementing original ideas and helping in others, with Three years experience in Web Development (Mainly back-end) resulting an award-winning work. Other experiences Include Robotics, Embedded Systems, ML and Software Development with +10 programming languages.

Beside applied experience, I'm an active member in the engineering community, being a mentor in NASA Space Apps Challenge Ismailia for two successive years, also giving a wide range of sessions and workshops in various topics including Engineering Mathematics, Android, Embedded Systems.

Serving as an active member in various non-profit entities, namely IEEE, Eureka Scientific Society and also co-founding Fab Lab Ismailia and IOnerds; this helped shaping the scientific community around, by organizing sessions, workshops, events and other engineering-related activities.

Current Interests are around Software Engineering, Embedded Systems, Blockchain and AI.
